Layout Options — Which One Feels Right?

Same story page shown 5 different ways. Pick your favorite!

Layout A — Image Left, Text Right (Book Spread)

Classic children's book spread. Image gets a full page, text faces it. Good for bilingual — plenty of room for both languages. This is how most KDP bilingual books work.

Day one: Ho-rang paced back and forth. "This garlic is TERRIBLE," he said.

Day ten: Ho-rang scratched the walls. "I MISS the sun," he growled.

Day twenty: Ho-rang's eyes went wide. "I CAN'T DO THIS!" he roared — and he burst out of the cave and ran into the bright, bright sunlight.

Gom-i watched him go. Her stomach hurt. Her eyes hurt. She missed the sky so much it made her chest ache.

But she stayed.
첫째 날: 호랑이 왔다 갔다 했어요. "이 마늘 정말 끔찍해!"

열째 날: 호랑이 벽을 긁었어요. "햇빛이 그리워!"

스무째 날: "못 참겠어!" 호랑이 포효하며 — 동굴 밖으로 뛰쳐나갔어요.

곰이는 호랑이 가는 모습을 지켜보았어요. 하지만 곰이는 남았어요.
